Assassin's Creed Mirage

Ubisoft Philippines

2023 | Technical Level Designer | Anvil Engine & C#

Contributed to the project from pre-production to closure (Aug 2021–Aug 2023) as part of the Philippines World/Content team, supporting teams across Singapore, Sofia, and Bordeaux. Worked on debugging and validating World Locations and Quests, implemented quest tech using visual scripting, developed C# tools in the Anvil engine to improve workflows, maintained documentation, and established a content pipeline for Southeast Asia to prevent critical issues during the project's final phase.

Pew Pew

Personal Project

2020 | Android | Solo Project | Unity & C#, Adobe Xd

Pew Pew is an infinite runner with shooter mechanics developed as a personal solo project for Android. The project served as a hands-on exercise to strengthen my skills in game development, UI design and implementation, and visual rendering. It also focused on exploring Unity’s Universal Render Pipeline (URP), with particular attention to post-processing and performance optimization.

The Painter

Thesis 1

Sync or Sink

ESGS: Game On 2018 Entry

2018 | Android Mobile AR, Tabletop | Project Manager, UI/UX Designer & Artist | Adobe Photoshop

Sync or Sink is an AR tabletop game that combines physical cards with an Android phone. Developed by a six-member team, it was submitted as an entry to ESGS: Game On 2018 and created alongside our thesis project, The Painter. I primarily served as the Project Manager, coordinating both projects simultaneously with the same team. Due to limited manpower, I also contributed as the primary UI/UX Designer and UI Artist for the project.
